Theatre Events

Mono Acting

  • Duration: 3+1 minutes (including entry and exit).
  • Language: English only.
  • Performance: Self-directed and self-written acts in monologue or mono-act style.
  • Props: Basic props and costumes encouraged for quality.
  • Background Music: Permitted, must be submitted to the tech team beforehand.
  • Finalists: Top 2 winners from prelims advance to finals (15-17 January 2025).

Theatre Events

Street Theatre

  • Duration: 8+2 minutes (including entry/exit).
  • Participants: Minimum 15 and maximum 25 members.
  • Language: English thematic with jingles in at least 4 Indian languages.
  • Prohibited: Loudspeakers, harmful materials, lifts between genders, etc.
  • Qualification: Based on number of participating teams; top 1-3 teams advance.

Theatre Events

Proscenium Theatre

  • Duration: 20+5 minutes (including stage setup and clearance).
  • Participants: Minimum 8 and maximum 25 (including backstage crew).
  • Language: English, Hindi, or Kannada; originals or adaptations allowed.
  • Provisions: Chairs, table, basic mics, and lighting provided.
  • Qualification: Based on number of participating teams; top 1-3 teams advance.

Theatre Events

Mime

  • Duration: 7+1 minutes (including stage entry and exit).
  • Participants: Maximum 10 members per team (minimum 6 on stage at all times).
  • Props: Not allowed; narration prohibited.
  • Sound: Recorded or live music allowed with minimal instruments.
  • Qualification: Top 1-3 teams advance based on number of participating teams.

Theatre Events

Short Film Making

  • Time: 6-8 minutes (intro, credits included).
  • Participants: Minimum 5 and maximum 15 (includes production team).
  • Language: English (10-15% regional allowed with subtitles).
  • Requirements: Must include specific props or phrases, shot on mobile phones only.
  • Criteria: Editing, story, acting, cinematography, and screenplay.
