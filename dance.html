Dance Events

Indian Dance Classical (Solo)

  • Time: 4+1 minutes on stage.
  • Song: Only authentic classical songs allowed, no mixing of songs.
  • Music: No live music allowed, no film songs.
  • Costumes: Authentic costumes, hairstyle, and jewelry representing the dance form.
  • Qualification: First 2 winners of the prelims advance to finals.
  • Deanery Rules: Deaneries with more than 5 participants to send 3 finalists.

Dance Events

Indian Dance Group: Non Theme (Film / Folk)

  • Time: 5+1 minutes on stage.
  • Music: Only Indian film songs allowed; no remixes or album songs.
  • Team Size: Min 8 and max 15 members.
  • Props: Minimum props allowed.
  • Qualification: Top 3 winning teams of the prelims to advance to finals.
  • Deanery Rules: Deaneries with more than 5 teams to send 3 finalists.
