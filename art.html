Art Events

Photography

  • Individual event.
  • Themes will be announced 1 day prior to the event.
  • Each student should submit 2 photos in JPEG format.
  • Basic editing (cropping, color correction) is allowed, advanced editing is prohibited.
  • Only DSLR photos are allowed.
  • Qualification: First and second winners from prelims advance to finals.
