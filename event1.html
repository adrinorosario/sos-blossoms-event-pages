Music Festival

Battle of Bands (Western)

  • Time: 10+2 minutes (setup & clearance included).
  • Minimum 5 and maximum 10 members per team.
  • No backing tracks, samples, or sequences allowed.
  • Standard 5-piece drum kit provided; no growling allowed.
  • Individual prizes: Best Original Composition, Vocalist, Keyboardist, Guitarist, etc.
  • Own compositions must submit lyrics in English for profanity check.

Music Festival

Acapella (Group)

  • Time: 10+2 minutes (setup & clearance included).
  • Minimum 5 and maximum 10 members per team (including optional Beatboxer).
  • No instruments or backup tracks allowed.
  • Individual prizes: Best Original Composition, Arrangement, Vocalist, Beatboxer.

Music Festival

Indian Group

  • Time: 10+2 minutes (setup & clearance included).
  • Team size: 5-10 members with at least one Indian melodic or percussion instrument.
  • Performances must be in any Indian language.
  • No growling, pre-recorded music, or samples allowed.
  • Own compositions require lyrics and translation for vulgarity check.

Music Festival

Indian Folk and Film Singing (Solo)

  • Time: 4+1 minutes.
  • One accompanist allowed with acoustic guitar or Indian percussion instruments.
  • No sequenced beats, samples, or backing tracks allowed.
  • Lyrics must be appropriate, grooming to meet University conduct.
